For Students

Student Resources

Everything students need to stay on track with their academics and school life.

01

Exam Schedule

Mid-term exams: October. Final exams: March. Board exams: As per board schedule. Date sheets are posted on the notice board 2 weeks before exams.

02

School Rules

Uniform is compulsory. Students must maintain 80% attendance. Mobile phones are not allowed on campus. Respect for teachers and fellow students is expected at all times.

03

Library

Open Monday to Friday, 9 AM – 3 PM. Students can borrow up to 2 books for 2 weeks. Reference section available for in-library use.

04

Sports & Clubs

Cricket, Football, Badminton, Table Tennis. Clubs: Science Club, Debate Club, Art Club, Naat & Qiraat. Join through your class teacher.

05

Transport

School van service available covering major routes in the city. Monthly charges: Rs. 1,500 – 2,500 depending on distance. Contact office for route details.

06

Scholarships

Merit-based scholarships for top 3 positions in each class. Need-based fee concession available. Apply through the principal's office.

For Parents

Parent Information

Staying connected with your child's education.

PTM

Parent-Teacher Meetings

Held monthly on the last Saturday. Individual meetings can be scheduled through the class teacher. Your involvement matters for your child's success.

FEE

Fee Payment

Fees are due by the 10th of each month. Payment accepted at the school office or via bank transfer. Late fee of Rs. 200 applies after the 15th.

RPT

Progress Reports

Report cards are issued after mid-term and final exams. Monthly progress reports sent home via diary. Check your child's diary daily for updates.

FB

Complaints & Suggestions

Drop a written complaint/suggestion at the office or email us at alimunawar100@yahoo.com. All concerns are addressed within 3 working days.

CAL

School Calendar

Academic year: April to March. Summer vacation: June 15 – Aug 14. Winter break: Dec 20 – Jan 1. Public holidays as per government schedule.

SEC

Safety & Security

CCTV cameras throughout campus. Security guard at all entry points. Students released only to authorized guardians. Emergency contact system in place.
